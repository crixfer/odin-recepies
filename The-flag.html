The Dominican Flag

This is a very typical Dominican food preferred for most. It has a delicious combination of flavors that make you feel in heaven.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • beans
  • meat
  • fried plantain
  • avocado

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Cook the beans.
  3. Cook preferred meat.
  4. Fried some ripe plantains.
  5. Serve avocado as you like.

Enjoy your delicious meal!
