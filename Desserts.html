Dominican Desserts

Quesillo

This is one of the most delicious desserts you are going to taste in your life. Trust me!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • milk
  • vanilla
  • sugar
  • water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sugar,
  2. mix and blend the water in the pan with the sugar,
  3. mix the eggs, the vanilla and the milk,
  4. cover the mold with the aforementioned items,
  5. boil in slow fire until ready.
